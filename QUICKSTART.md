Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser

## 4. Customize Your Data
Update these files with your information:
- `data/projects.ts` - Your projects
- `data/experience.ts` - Your work experience

- `data/certifications.ts` - Your certifications

Place your images in:
- `public/images/projects/` - Project screenshots
- `public/images/certifications/` - Certification images
- `public/images/experience/` - Company photos

## 6. Build for Production
```bash
npm run build
```

## 7. Deploy
The `out/` folder contains your static website ready for deployment!
